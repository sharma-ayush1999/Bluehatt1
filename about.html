Connecting has never been easier.

With magicJack you can connect with friends and family using your high-speed internet. Whether you're using your magicJack at home, or traveling abroad (you can take it with you), you can always rely on your magicJack to put you in touch with your loved ones.

How magicJack Works

The magicJack device uses Voice Over Internet Protocol (“VoIP”) to allow you to receive unlimited calls from the U.S. and Canada using an existing internet connection. You can communicate using your computer or regular telephone. No additional telephone service is required.

Connect with High-Speed Modem

magicJack is easy to set up with your existing high-speed internet connection and your telephone. You are only a few quick steps away from receiving ultra-low cost internet phone calls.

  1. Using the provided Ethernet cord, connect the router and your magicJack.
  2. Plug your phone into the port labeled "PHONE" on your magicJack.
  3. Connect the provided power adapter to your magicJack USB.
  4. Plug your magicJack into a power outlet.
Connect with Your Computer

magicJack is easy to set up with your computer. Use your telephone or the magicJack desktop application to receive calls.

  1. Plug your phone into the port labeled "PHONE" on your magicJack.
  2. [OPTIONAL] Connect the provided USB extension to your magicJack.
  3. Plug your magicJack into a USB port on your computer.
